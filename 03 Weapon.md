# 03 Weapon

This tool is designed for adding fire effects to any static mesh, not just weapons. You can easily attach additional fire sprites to enhance your objects.

## Step 1
Add Weapon Static Mesh to Skeletal Mesh and attach to its parent socket.

Add Niagara System (Weapon Fire VFX) to the static mesh.

<img width="1910" height="947" alt="Weapon_AddNiagara" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/11c82d6b-99bd-4f95-95d2-bb44572bbceb" />

## Step 3
Adjust the parameters to make the Fire suits the weapon.

<img width="1920" height="953" alt="Weapon_AdjustParameters" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/893918ed-6060-4e01-b39e-c04c61f863de" />
